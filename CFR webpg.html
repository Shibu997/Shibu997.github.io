ABOUT US

The Newlife Hospital and Research Centre is a private hospital located in Bandra, Mumbai, India. The hospital was established in 1978 by the Lilavati Kirtilal Mehta Medical Trust.In 1997, the trust established Newlife Hospital, with 314 beds, 12 operating theaters, 300 consultants, intensive care units (ICUs), and 1,800 staff members to provide care for 300 in-patients and 1,500 out-patients daily.With the motto "More Than Healthcare, Human Care", the hospital has the mission of providing affordable, high-quality health care

Accreditations

The National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH), has recently accredited Lilavati Hospital as a provider of medical treatment that meets high standards and utilizes the latest medical equipment, such as the fastest CT scanners.[4] NABH considered the level of treatment for different patients, the standard of control and prevention, and the responsiveness of the intensive care unit as key features that have matched NABH standards of accreditation. The hospital offers treatment in nuclear medicine and cardiac imaging, general surgery,pediatrics, interventional cardiology, gynecology and obstetrics, rehabilitative services, scans.
